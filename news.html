News

Donnie recovers from fall

Good news! Our bassist Donnie Walsh, who fell off the stage at a gig at the Hexham Cider Festival, is out of hospital and expected to make a full recovery. The band want to thank the fans for all their support and wellwishes during this difficult time.

New Member Joins The Weavers

We'd like to welcome Garry Miller to the band. Garry is an extremely talented Saxophonist and will bring an extra dimension to our live shows with his great playing.

Greatest Hits album released in September!

The Weavers Greatest Hits, a collection of the band's twenty biggest floor fillers will be realeased on 21st September. The album will feature material from 1963 all the way up to the present day.

Extra summer gigs announced

The Weavers are delighted to announce a further two gigs in June and July. A full list of upcoming concerts is shown below:
  • Leeds Cotton Club - 18th June 2019
  • Hull Bongo Club - 28th June 2019
  • Newcastle SECC - 3rd July 2019
  • Berwick Upon Tweed Citrus Rooms - 15th July 2019
